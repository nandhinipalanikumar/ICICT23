ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

           International Conference on Innovation in Computing & Technology (ICICT-2023) is aimed to promote the scientific platform for exchange of ideas between the researchers and promoting innovation aspects in Engineering & Science. The conference will boost excellent innovations in terms of day life and academics. This International Conference on Innovation in Computing & Technology is Organized on 31st March 2023 by Velammal Institute of Technology, Chennai, India. This Conference is an initiation to build a technical society for future collaboration and specific goal of globalization to achieve international standards.
